Opening of a new educational institution - “Aqbobek Lyceum”

Dear Parents, After four years of successful operation of Aqbobek International School, we noticed a need for a specialized private school for talented children.

How was it formed?

After careful discussion of this issue, we decided to open a new educational institution - “Aqbobek Lyceum”, where talented children from all over the country will study.

What's the Aqbobek Lyceum?

“Aqbobek Lyceum is a boarding school for students in grades 7-10 with a focus on science and technology. A boarding school will be provided for non-resident students. Admission to the school will be competitive based on entrance exams in math and reading literacy.

We will be posting more information about the school and the admissions process soon. The Aqbobek Lyceum will open its doors on September 1, 2024, and it may be your child who becomes part of the Aqbobek family
