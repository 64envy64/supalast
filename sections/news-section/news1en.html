Successful Hackathon at Aqbobek International School: AISHack '24

For 24 hours, from February 17 to 18, 2024, Aqbobek International School became an epicenter of creativity and innovation, hosting one of the most exciting events in the world of technology - hackathon.

The event, which brings together young programmers, designers and tech gurus, has created a unique platform for students to develop their skills and creative thinking.

Not just any "hackathon"

The hackathon, organized on the basis of Aqbobek International School, was a continuous 24-hour marathon, during which participants overcame technical obstacles, searched for innovative solutions and put them into practice. The participants, organized in teams of 2-4 people, started working on their projects with enthusiasm and determination. During this time, the school corridors were filled with the sounds of code, discussions and creative ideas, and the air was saturated with an atmosphere of competition and mutual support.

The best for the best

The event attracted more than 60 students from 18 schools in Aktobe, representing a variety of talents and creativity The terms of reference for the hackathon were clear and challenging: to create a game based on the Python programming language with a Kazakh identity. This requirement stimulated participants to be creative and seek inspiration from the rich culture and traditions of Kazakhstan

After a busy development period, it was time for presentations. Each team demonstrated their game and explained its concept and features. Not only technical skills were evaluated, but also the originality and creativity of the idea, as well as its relevance to Kazakh culture and traditions After careful consideration and discussion, the jury determined the winners.
